Genesis

The term Genesis means birth, origin, or in the beginning. In this book of the Bible you can read about many beginnings: beginning of the universe, of man and woman, the sin of man, the beginning of the promises of God regarding salvation, and the beginning of Israel as God's chosen people. The Book of Genesis contains stories of some of the popular biblical characters: Adam and Eve, Abraham, Isaac, Jacob, Joseph.
