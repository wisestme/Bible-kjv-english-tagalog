1 And it came to pass afterward, that he went throughout every city and village, preaching and shewing the glad tidings of the kingdom of God: and the twelve were with him,1 At nangyari pagkatapos ng kaunting panahon, na siya'y naglalakad sa mga bayan at mga nayon, na ipinangangaral at dinadala ang mabubuting balita ng kaharian ng Dios, at kasama niya ang labingdalawa,
2 And certain women, which had been healed of evil spirits and infirmities, Mary called Magdalene, out of whom went seven devils,2 At ang ilang babae na pinagaling sa masasamang espiritu at sa mga sakit, si Maria, na tinatawag na Magdalena, na sa kaniya'y pitong demonio ang nagsilabas,
3 And Joanna the wife of Chuza Herod's steward, and Susanna, and many others, which ministered unto him of their substance.3 At si Juana na asawa ni Chuza, katiwala ni Herodes, at si Susana, at iba pang marami na ipinaglilingkod sa kanila ang kanilang tinatangkilik.
4 And when much people were gathered together, and were come to him out of every city, he spake by a parable:4 At nang magkatipon ang malaking karamihang tao, at ang mga mula sa bawa't bayan na nagsadya sa kaniya, ay nagsalita siya sa pamamagitan ng isang talinghaga:
5 A sower went out to sow his seed: and as he sowed, some fell by the way side; and it was trodden down, and the fowls of the air devoured it.5 Ang manghahasik ay yumaon upang maghasik ng kaniyang binhi: at sa kaniyang paghahasik ang ilan ay nangahulog sa tabi ng daan; at napagyapakan, at ito'y kinain ng mga ibon sa langit.
6 And some fell upon a rock; and as soon as it was sprung up, it withered away, because it lacked moisture.6 At ang iba'y nahulog sa batuhan; at pagsibol, ay natuyo, sapagka't walang halumigmig.
7 And some fell among thorns; and the thorns sprang up with it, and choked it.7 At ang iba'y nahulog sa mga dawagan; at tumubong kasama ang mga dawag, at yao'y ininis.
8 And other fell on good ground, and sprang up, and bare fruit an hundredfold. And when he had said these things, he cried, He that hath ears to hear, let him hear.8 At ang iba'y nahulog sa mabuting lupa, at tumubo at nagbunga ng tigiisang daan. Pagkasabi niya ng mga bagay na ito, siya ay sumigaw, Ang may mga pakinig na ipakikinig, ay makinig.
9 And his disciples asked him, saying, What might this parable be?9 At tinanong siya ng kaniyang mga alagad, kung ano kaya ang talinghagang ito.
10 And he said, Unto you it is given to know the mysteries of the kingdom of God: but to others in parables; that seeing they might not see, and hearing they might not understand.10 At sinabi niya, Sa inyo'y ipinagkaloob ang mangakaalam ng mga hiwaga ng kaharian ng Dios: datapuwa't sa mga iba'y sa mga talinghaga; upang kung magsitingin ay huwag silang mangakakita, at mangakarinig ay huwag silang mangakaunawa.
11 Now the parable is this: The seed is the word of God.11 Ito ang talinghaga: Ang binhi ay ang salita ng Dios.
12 Those by the way side are they that hear; then cometh the devil, and taketh away the word out of their hearts, lest they should believe and be saved.12 At ang mga sa tabi ng daan, ay ang nangakinig; kung magkagayo'y dumarating ang diablo, at inaalis ang salita sa kanilang puso, upang huwag silang magsisampalataya at mangaligtas.
13 They on the rock are they, which, when they hear, receive the word with joy; and these have no root, which for a while believe, and in time of temptation fall away.13 At ang mga sa batuhan, ay yaong mga pagkarinig, ay tinatanggap na may galak ang salita; at ang mga ito'y walang ugat, na sila sa sangdaling panaho'y nagsisisampalataya, at sa panahon ng tukso ay nagsisihiwalay.
14 And that which fell among thorns are they, which, when they have heard, go forth, and are choked with cares and riches and pleasures of this life, and bring no fruit to perfection.14 At ang nahulog sa dawagan, ay ito ang nangakinig, at samantalang sila'y nagsisiyaon sa kanilang lakad ay iniinis sila ng mga pagsusumakit at mga kayamanan at mga kalayawan sa buhay na ito, at hindi nangagbubunga ng kasakdalan.
15 But that on the good ground are they, which in an honest and good heart, having heard the word, keep it, and bring forth fruit with patience.15 At ang sa mabuting lupa ay ang mga pusong timtiman at mabuti, na iniingatan ang salita pagkarinig, at nangagbubunga may pagtitiis.
16 No man, when he hath lighted a candle, covereth it with a vessel, or putteth it under a bed; but setteth it on a candlestick, that they which enter in may see the light.16 At walang taong pagkapaningas niya ng ilawan ay tinatakpan ng isang banga, o inilalagay kaya ito sa ilalim ng isang higaan; kundi inilalagay ito sa talagang lalagyan, upang makita ng nagsisipasok ang ilaw.
17 For nothing is secret, that shall not be made manifest; neither any thing hid, that shall not be known and come abroad.17 Sapagka't walang bagay na natatago, na di mahahayag; o walang lihim, na di makikilala at mapapasa liwanag.
18 Take heed therefore how ye hear: for whosoever hath, to him shall be given; and whosoever hath not, from him shall be taken even that which he seemeth to have.18 Ingatan ninyo kung paano ang inyong pakikinig: sapagka't sino mang mayroon ay bibigyan; at ang sinomang wala, pati ng inaakala niyang nasa kaniya ay aalisin.
19 Then came to him his mother and his brethren, and could not come at him for the press.19 At nagsiparoon sa kaniya ang kaniyang ina at mga kapatid, at sila'y hindi mangakalapit sa kaniya dahil sa karamihan ng tao.
20 And it was told him by certain which said, Thy mother and thy brethren stand without, desiring to see thee.20 At may nagsabi sa kaniya, Nangakatayo sa labas ang iyong ina at iyong mga kapatid, na ibig nilang makita ka.
21 And he answered and said unto them, My mother and my brethren are these which hear the word of God, and do it.21 Datapuwa't siya'y sumagot, at sinabi sa kanila, Ang aking ina at ang aking mga kapatid ay itong nangakikinig ng salita ng Dios, at ginagawa.
22 Now it came to pass on a certain day, that he went into a ship with his disciples: and he said unto them, Let us go over unto the other side of the lake. And they launched forth.22 Nangyari nga sa mga araw na yaon, na siya'y lumulan sa isang daong, siya at ang kaniyang mga alagad; at sinabi niya sa kanila, Magsitawid tayo sa kabilang ibayo ng dagatdagatan: at sila'y nagsitulak.
23 But as they sailed he fell asleep: and there came down a storm of wind on the lake; and they were filled with water, and were in jeopardy.23 Datapuwa't samantalang sila'y nangaglalayag, siya'y nakatulog: at bumugso ang isang unos ng hangin sa dagatdagatan; at sila'y nangatitigib ng tubig, at nangasa kapanganiban.
24 And they came to him, and awoke him, saying, Master, master, we perish. Then he arose, and rebuked the wind and the raging of the water: and they ceased, and there was a calm.24 At sila'y nangagsilapit sa kaniya at siya'y ginising, na nangagsasabi, Guro, guro, tayo'y mangamamatay. At siya'y gumising, at sinaway ang hangin at ang galit ng tubig: at nangagsitigil, at humusay ang panahon.
25 And he said unto them, Where is your faith? And they being afraid wondered, saying one to another, What manner of man is this! for he commandeth even the winds and water, and they obey him.25 At sinabi niya sa kanila, Saan naroon ang inyong pananampalataya? At palibhasa'y nangatakot sila'y nagsisipanggilalas, na sinasabi ng isa sa iba, Sino nga ito, na siya'y naguutos maging sa hangin at sa tubig, at siya'y tinatalima nila?
26 And they arrived at the country of the Gadarenes, which is over against Galilee.26 At sila'y nagsidating sa lupain ng mga Gadareno, na nasa tapat ng Galilea.
27 And when he went forth to land, there met him out of the city a certain man, which had devils long time, and ware no clothes, neither abode in any house, but in the tombs.27 At pagkalunsad niya sa lupa, siya'y sinalubong ng isang lalaking galing sa bayan, na may mga demonio; at malaong panahon na hindi siya nagdaramit, at hindi tumatahan sa bahay, kundi sa mga libingan.
28 When he saw Jesus, he cried out, and fell down before him, and with a loud voice said, What have I to do with thee, Jesus, thou Son of God most high? I beseech thee, torment me not.28 At nang makita niya si Jesus, siya'y sumigaw, at nagpatirapa sa harap niya, at sinabi ng malakas na tinig, Ano ang pakialam ko sa iyo, Jesus, ikaw na Anak ng Dios na Kataastaasan? Ipinamamanhik ko sa iyo, na huwag mo akong pahirapan.
29 (For he had commanded the unclean spirit to come out of the man. For oftentimes it had caught him: and he was kept bound with chains and in fetters; and he brake the bands, and was driven of the devil into the wilderness.)29 Sapagka't ipinagutos niya sa karumaldumal na espiritu na lumabas sa tao. Sapagka't madalas siyang inaalihan: at siya'y binabantayan at gapos ng mga tanikala at mga damal; at pagka pinapatid ang gapos ay siya'y itinataboy ng demonio sa mga ilang.
30 And Jesus asked him, saying, What is thy name? And he said, Legion: because many devils were entered into him.30 At tinanong siya ni Jesus, Ano ang pangalan mo? At sinabi niya, Pulutong; sapagka't maraming demonio ang nagsipasok sa kaniya.
31 And they besought him that he would not command them to go out into the deep.31 At ipinamamanhik nila sa kaniya na huwag silang paparoonin sa kalalimlaliman.
32 And there was there an herd of many swine feeding on the mountain: and they besought him that he would suffer them to enter into them. And he suffered them.32 Doon nga'y may isang kawan ng maraming baboy na nagsisipanginain sa bundok: at ipinamanhik nila sa kaniya na pabayaang sila'y magsipasok sa mga yaon. At sila'y pinahintulutan niya.
33 Then went the devils out of the man, and entered into the swine: and the herd ran violently down a steep place into the lake, and were choked.33 At nagsilabas ang mga demonio sa tao, at nagsipasok sa mga baboy: at ang kawan ay napadaluhong sa bangin hanggang sa dagatdagatan, at nangalunod.
34 When they that fed them saw what was done, they fled, and went and told it in the city and in the country.34 At nang makita ng nagsisipagalaga ng mga yaon ang nangyari, ay nagsitakas, at isinaysay sa bayan at sa bukid.
35 Then they went out to see what was done; and came to Jesus, and found the man, out of whom the devils were departed, sitting at the feet of Jesus, clothed, and in his right mind: and they were afraid.35 At sila'y nagsilabas upang makita ang nangyari; at nagsilapit sila kay Jesus, at kanilang naratnan sa paanan ni Jesus ang taong nilabasan ng mga demonio, na nakaupo, may pananamit, at matino ang kaniyang bait: at sila'y nangatakot.
36 They also which saw it told them by what means he that was possessed of the devils was healed.36 At sa kanila'y isinaysay ng nangakakita kung paanong pinagaling ang inaalihan ng mga demonio.
37 Then the whole multitude of the country of the Gadarenes round about besought him to depart from them; for they were taken with great fear: and he went up into the ship, and returned back again.37 At ipinamanhik sa kaniya ng lahat ng mga tao sa palibotlibot ng lupain ng mga Gadareno na siya'y umalis sa kanila, sapagka't sila'y napipigilan ng malaking takot: at siya'y lumulan sa daong, at nagbalik.
38 Now the man out of whom the devils were departed besought him that he might be with him: but Jesus sent him away, saying,38 Datapuwa't namanhik sa kaniya ang taong nilabasan ng mga demonio, na siya'y ipagsama niya: datapuwa't siya'y pinaalis niya, na sinasabi,
39 Return to thine own house, and shew how great things God hath done unto thee. And he went his way, and published throughout the whole city how great things Jesus had done unto him.39 Umuwi ka sa iyong bahay, at ipahayag mo kung gaano kadakilang mga bagay ang ginawa ng Dios sa iyo. At siya'y yumaon ng kaniyang lakad, na ibinabalita sa buong bayan, kung gaanong kadakilang mga bagay ang sa kaniya'y ginawa ni Jesus.
40 And it came to pass, that, when Jesus was returned, the people gladly received him: for they were all waiting for him.40 At sa pagbalik ni Jesus, ay sinalubong siyang may galak ng karamihan; sapagka't hinihintay siya nilang lahat.
41 And, behold, there came a man named Jairus, and he was a ruler of the synagogue: and he fell down at Jesus' feet, and besought him that he would come into his house:41 At narito, lumapit ang isang lalaking nagngangalang Jairo, at siya'y isang pinuno sa sinagoga: at siya'y nagpatirapa sa paanan ni Jesus, at ipinamamanhik sa kaniya, na pumasok sa kaniyang bahay;
42 For he had one only daughter, about twelve years of age, and she lay a dying. But as he went the people thronged him.42 Sapagka't siya'y may isang bugtong na anak na babae, na may labingdalawang taong gulang, at siya'y naghihingalo. Datapuwa't samantalang siya'y lumalakad ay sinisiksik siya ng karamihan.
43 And a woman having an issue of blood twelve years, which had spent all her living upon physicians, neither could be healed of any,43 At isang babae na may labingdalawang taon nang inaagasan, na ginugol sa mga manggagamot ang lahat niyang pagkabuhay, at sinoma'y walang makapagpagaling sa kaniya,
44 Came behind him, and touched the border of his garment: and immediately her issue of blood stanched.44 Ay lumapit sa kaniyang likuran, at hinipo ang laylayan ng kaniyang damit at pagdaka'y naampat ang kaniyang agas.
45 And Jesus said, Who touched me? When all denied, Peter and they that were with him said, Master, the multitude throng thee and press thee, and sayest thou, Who touched me?45 At sinabi ni Jesus, Sino ang humipo sa akin? At nang tumatanggi ang lahat, ay sinabi ni Pedro, at ng mga kasamahan niya, Guro, iniimpit ka at sinisiksik ka ng karamihan.
46 And Jesus said, Somebody hath touched me: for I perceive that virtue is gone out of me.46 Datapuwa't sinabi ni Jesus, May humipo sa akin, sapagka't naramdaman ko na may umalis na bisa sa akin.
47 And when the woman saw that she was not hid, she came trembling, and falling down before him, she declared unto him before all the people for what cause she had touched him, and how she was healed immediately.47 At nang makita ng babae na siya'y hindi nalingid, ay lumapit siya na nangangatal, at nagpatirapa sa harapan niya na isinasaysay sa harapan ng buong bayan ang dahil kung bakit siya'y hinipo niya, at kung paanong gumaling siya kapagdaka.
48 And he said unto her, Daughter, be of good comfort: thy faith hath made thee whole; go in peace.48 At sinabi niya sa kaniya, Anak, pinagaling ka ng iyong pananampalataya; yumaon kang payapa.
49 While he yet spake, there cometh one from the ruler of the synagogue's house, saying to him, Thy daughter is dead; trouble not the Master.49 Samantalang nagsasalita pa siya ay dumating ang isa na mula sa bahay ng pinuno sa sinagoga, na nagsasabi, Patay na ang anak mong babae; huwag mong bagabagin ang Guro.
50 But when Jesus heard it, he answered him, saying, Fear not: believe only, and she shall be made whole.50 Datapuwa't nang marinig ito ni Jesus ay sumagot sa kaniya, Huwag kang matakot: manampalataya ka lamang, at siya'y gagaling.
51 And when he came into the house, he suffered no man to go in, save Peter, and James, and John, and the father and the mother of the maiden.51 At nang dumating siya sa bahay, ay hindi niya ipinahintulot na pumasok na kasama niya ang sinomang tao, maliban na kay Pedro, at kay Juan, at kay Santiago, at ang ama ng dalaga at ang ina nito.
52 And all wept, and bewailed her: but he said, Weep not; she is not dead, but sleepeth.52 At tumatangis ang lahat, at pinananambitanan ang dalaga: datapuwa't sinabi niya, Huwag kayong magsitangis; sapagka't siya'y hindi patay, kundi natutulog.
53 And they laughed him to scorn, knowing that she was dead.53 At tinawanan nila siya na nililibak, na napagaalamang siya'y patay na.
54 And he put them all out, and took her by the hand, and called, saying, Maid, arise.54 Datapuwa't tinangnan niya sa kamay, at tinawag, na sinasabi, Dalaga, magbangon ka.
55 And her spirit came again, and she arose straightway: and he commanded to give her meat.55 At nagbalik ang kaniyang espiritu, at siya'y nagbangon pagdaka: at kaniyang ipinagutos na siya'y bigyan ng pagkain.
56 And her parents were astonished: but he charged them that they should tell no man what was done.56 At nangagtaka ang kaniyang mga magulang: datapuwa't ipinagbilin niya sa kanila na huwag sabihin kanino man ang kaniyang ginawa.
