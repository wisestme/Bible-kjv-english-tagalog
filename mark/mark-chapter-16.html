Chapter 16 - Mark

1 And when the sabbath was past, Mary Magdalene, and Mary the mother of James, and Salome, had bought sweet spices, that they might come and anoint him.1 At nang makaraan ang sabbath, si Maria Magdalena, at si Mariang ina ni Santiago, at si Salome, ay nagsibili ng mga pabango, upang sila'y magsiparoon at siya'y pahiran.
2 And very early in the morning the first day of the week, they came unto the sepulchre at the rising of the sun.2 At pagkaumagang-umaga nang unang araw ng sanglinggo, ay nagsiparoon sila sa libingan nang sikat na ang araw.
3 And they said among themselves, Who shall roll us away the stone from the door of the sepulchre?3 At kanilang pinaguusapan, Sino kaya ang ating mapagpapagulong ng bato mula sa pintuan ng libingan?
4 And when they looked, they saw that the stone was rolled away: for it was very great.4 At pagkatingin, ay nakita nilang naigulong na ang bato: sapagka't yao'y totoong malaki.
5 And entering into the sepulchre, they saw a young man sitting on the right side, clothed in a long white garment; and they were affrighted.5 At pagkapasok sa libingan, ay kanilang nakita ang isang binata na nakaupo sa dakong kanan, na nararamtan ng isang damit na maputi; at sila'y nangagitla.
6 And he saith unto them, Be not affrighted: Ye seek Jesus of Nazareth, which was crucified: he is risen; he is not here: behold the place where they laid him.6 At sinabi niya sa kanila, Huwag kayong mangagitla: hinahanap ninyo si Jesus, ang Nazareno, na ipinako sa krus: siya'y nagbangon; wala siya rito: tingnan ninyo ang dakong pinaglagyan nila sa kaniya!
7 But go your way, tell his disciples and Peter that he goeth before you into Galilee: there shall ye see him, as he said unto you.7 Datapuwa't magsiyaon kayo, sabihin ninyo sa kaniyang mga alagad at kay Pedro, Siya'y nangunguna sa inyo sa Galilea: doon siya makikita, ayon sa sinabi niya sa inyo.
8 And they went out quickly, and fled from the sepulchre; for they trembled and were amazed: neither said they any thing to any man; for they were afraid.8 At sila'y nagsilabas, at nagsitakas mula sa libingan; sapagka't sila'y nagsipangilabot at nangagitla: at hindi sila nangagsasabi ng anoman sa kanino man; sapagka't sila'y nangatatakot.
9 Now when Jesus was risen early the first day of the week, he appeared first to Mary Magdalene, out of whom he had cast seven devils.9 Nang siya nga'y magbangon nang unang araw ng sanglinggo, ay napakita muna siya kay Maria Magdalena, na sa kaniya'y pitong demonio ang pinalabas niya.
10 And she went and told them that had been with him, as they mourned and wept.10 Siya'y yumaon at ipinagbigay alam sa mga naging kasamahan ni Jesus, samantalang sila'y nangahahapis at nagsisitangis.
11 And they, when they had heard that he was alive, and had been seen of her, believed not.11 At sila, nang kanilang mabalitaan na siya'y nabuhay, at nakita ni Magdalena, ay hindi sila nagsipaniwala.
12 After that he appeared in another form unto two of them, as they walked, and went into the country.12 At pagkatapos ng mga bagay na ito ay napakita siya sa ibang anyo sa dalawa sa kanila, nang sila'y nangaglalakad na patungo sa bukid.
13 And they went and told it unto the residue: neither believed they them.13 At sila'y nagsiyaon at ipinagbigay-alam ito sa mga iba: at kahit sa kanila'y hindi rin sila nagsipaniwala.
14 Afterward he appeared unto the eleven as they sat at meat, and upbraided them with their unbelief and hardness of heart, because they believed not them which had seen him after he was risen.14 At pagkatapos siya'y napakita sa labingisa samantalang sila'y nangakaupong nagsisikain; at pinagwikaan sila sa kawalan nila ng pananampalataya at katigasan ng puso, sapagka't hindi sila nagsipaniwala sa nangakakita sa kaniya pagkatapos na siya'y magbangon.
15 And he said unto them, Go ye into all the world, and preach the gospel to every creature.15 At sinabi niya sa kanila, Magsiyaon kayo sa buong sanglibutan, at inyong ipangaral ang evangelio sa lahat ng kinapal.
16 He that believeth and is baptized shall be saved; but he that believeth not shall be damned.16 Ang sumasampalataya at mabautismuhan ay maliligtas; datapuwa't ang hindi sumasampalataya ay parurusahan.
17 And these signs shall follow them that believe; In my name shall they cast out devils; they shall speak with new tongues;17 At lalakip ang mga tandang ito sa magsisisampalataya: mangagpapalabas sila ng mga demonio sa aking pangalan; mangagsasalita sila ng mga bagong wika;
18 They shall take up serpents; and if they drink any deadly thing, it shall not hurt them; they shall lay hands on the sick, and they shall recover.18 Sila'y magsisihawak ng mga ahas, at kung magsiinom sila ng bagay na makamamatay, sa anomang paraan ay hindi makasasama sa kanila; ipapatong nila ang kanilang mga kamay sa mga may-sakit, at sila'y magsisigaling.
19 So then after the Lord had spoken unto them, he was received up into heaven, and sat on the right hand of God.19 Ang Panginoong Jesus nga, pagkatapos na sila'y mangakausap niya, ay tinanggap sa itaas ng langit, at lumuklok sa kanan ng Dios.
20 And they went forth, and preached every where, the Lord working with them, and confirming the word with signs following. Amen.20 At nagsialis sila, at nagsipangaral sa lahat ng dako, na gumagawang kasama nila ang Panginoon, at pinatototohanan ang salita sa pamamagitan ng mga tandang kalakip. Siya nawa.
