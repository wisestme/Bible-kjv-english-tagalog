Psalms

In the book of Psalms, Israel praises, prays to, and ponders God in poems.

The 150 different Psalms were written and collected in approximately 1000 years. The organizations is technical and demonstrates how the Hebrews are serious with their hymn books and how they use it often.

Trivia: Moses wrote Psalms chapter 90.
